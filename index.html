"Chutiya Banaya Tumko: A Masterclass in the Art of Sass"

Have you ever experienced a moment in life so perfectly snarky, so profoundly filled with mic-drop energy, that you couldn’t help but marvel at the sheer audacity of it? If not, fret not. This image has got your back!

Meet Uncle Jee—a man who has single-handedly turned the simple act of delivering a verbal roast into an art form. You can almost hear him say, “Beta, aapne MBA kiya, aur humne zindagi. Guess who wins?”

The text below the image—"Chutiya Banaya Tumko"—isn’t just a statement. It’s a philosophy, a gentle reminder of how life, bosses, or your very own best friends can sometimes hand you a proverbial L.

A Day in the Life of ‘CBT’ Uncle

Picture this: Uncle starts his day with a perfectly brewed cup of chai. Not because he loves tea, but because he knows chai is the fuel for savage comebacks. By 10 AM, he’s already leveled half the neighborhood with subtle burns. By lunchtime, he’s done giving free gyaan about how he predicted the stock market crash (with zero evidence), all while making you question your own intelligence.

By the evening, he’s perched on his usual spot—most likely the head of a dining table or the village panchayat—delivering his iconic dialogues. His victims? Whoever made the grave mistake of crossing his path that day.
